ENSEMBLE_MODELS ¬∑ CUSTOMER_SEGMENTATION ¬∑ TABLEAU_DASHBOARDS

// CHALLENGE

Orange Telecom needed to identify high-risk customers likely to churn and understand the key drivers of customer attrition. With 3,300+ customer records spanning usage patterns, service plans, and customer service interactions, manual analysis couldn't provide the predictive accuracy needed for effective retention campaigns.

// SOLUTION

Built comprehensive analytics solution combining machine learning classification models with interactive Tableau dashboards. Analyzed customer behavior across multiple dimensions including account tenure, international plans, voicemail usage, customer service calls, and usage patterns (day/evening/night/international).

  • Engineered 20+ features including usage ratios, charge-per-minute metrics, and tenure groupings
  • Developed ensemble classification model (Random Forest + Gradient Boosting) achieving 88% accuracy
  • Created risk segmentation framework with 5 customer tiers: Very High, High, Medium, Low, Very Low risk
  • Built dual Tableau dashboards: Churn Analysis (predictive metrics) and Customer Segmentation (behavioral clustering)
  • Identified key churn drivers: customer service calls, international plan status, account length, and usage patterns
  • Calculated estimated revenue impact and monthly revenue per customer segment

// KEY INSIGHTS

  • Customers with 4+ service calls show 300% higher churn rate
  • International plan subscribers have distinct churn patterns requiring targeted interventions
  • High day-usage customers (>250 minutes) exhibit different retention needs
  • State-level analysis revealed geographic churn hotspots for localized campaigns
  • Tenure segmentation enabled lifecycle-based retention strategies
